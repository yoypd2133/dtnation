Pre-order Downtownation

Downtownation is an upcoming indie horror game that immerses players in a dark, terrifying world. The game is set in eerie, decaying environments where danger lurks around every corner, creating a sense of isolation and fear.

A standout feature of Downtownation is its camera mechanic, allowing players to use surveillance or hand-held cameras to track movements, monitor threats, and uncover hidden dangers. This mechanic adds a strategic element to the gameplay, as players must balance exploration with carefully watching for lurking enemies.
